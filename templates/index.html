Hate Speech Detector



Hate speech refers to any speech or action that incites violence or prejudicial behavior towards an individual or group, based on their race, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity, or other characteristic. It is not protected under the First Amendment and can vary by country. Combating hate speech requires education, promoting diversity and inclusion, and policies and laws that protect against discrimination.The main goal of this project is to find hate speech from given text.This tool give result as hate speech detected or not.
